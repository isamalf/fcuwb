About Our Club

Building financial literacy and career skills for UW Bothell students

Our Mission

The UW Bothell Finance Club is dedicated to providing students with opportunities to explore the world of finance, connect with industry professionals, and develop the skills needed for successful careers.

Through workshops, networking events, and educational sessions, we help students bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world financial practice.

Financial Education

Learn about markets, investments, and financial concepts through workshops and educational sessions.

Professional Networking

Connect with peers, alumni, and professionals in the finance industry.

Career Development

Develop practical skills and explore career opportunities in finance.
